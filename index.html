Premier Medical Laboratory Services
2007 – 2023
Flagship diagnostics laboratory · Greenville, SC
The cornerstone venture. A 40,000 sqft CLIA-certified laboratory at 6000 Pelham Road in Greenville, South Carolina. Built over 15 years into South Carolina's leading commercial clinical reference laboratory — and, in 2020, the first private lab in the state to receive FDA validation for COVID-19 testing.
  • First FDA-validated commercial COVID lab in South Carolina (March 2020)
  • Scaled to up to 300,000 tests per day with pooling
  • Yale SalivaDirect designated testing laboratory
  • DiabetesPredict exclusive US provider (GenomeWeb, June 2019)
  • 21,000 COVID variant sequences per week — genomic surveillance program
  • First lab to detect BA.2 and BA.4 variants in Florida (2022)
  • Blue Cross Blue Shield preferred COVID lab in TX, SC, and NC
  • HHS surge testing site in SC, AZ, UT, and NC
  • Praised by US Senator Lindsey Graham at a televised press conference (June 2021)

Infrastructure

6000 Pelham Road · Greenville, SC

Physical infrastructure matters. When COVID hit, we had a 40,000 sqft validated laboratory with a team already in place. That's why we were first.

The lab at 6000 Pelham Road is more than an address — it's the reason we could move faster than any competitor in the state when the pandemic hit. A validated CLIA laboratory, a PhD-led R&D bench, a trained operator team, and the redundant capacity to scale to hundreds of thousands of tests per day with pooling.

That infrastructure didn't appear in 2020. It was built brick by brick over 15 years, starting in 2007, so that when the moment came, we didn't have to wait. We ran.

Today the facility continues to support diagnostic operations, and its lessons are embedded in every new AI-powered healthcare platform being built today.
